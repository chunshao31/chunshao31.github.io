introduction of MOBA games

MOBA is abbreviation of multiplayer online battle arena.There are two teams,each team usually has five persons.The players can choose different roles,and they can attack neutral monsters and to upgrade,the goal of MOBA games is to destroy their opponents' main structure

introduction of some popular MOBA games on PC

LOL

League of Legend is one of the most popular MOBA games now.Since 2009,many persons have chosen to play this game.And LOL is the world's largest esport,it has a mature competitive system over the world. And over 100 million persons have watched.
