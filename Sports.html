Sport game is a video game or computer game that allows players to simulate participation in professional sports.


Sports games come in two main varieties: simulations and arcade sports games.
Both of these sub genres are focused on emulating the play experience of “real world” sports. The difference, however, comes in the execution. Simulations focus on realistic recreations of sport.
